Frequently Asked Questions

What is JagrukSeva ?
JagrukSeva is an e-Marketplace for Govt. schemes and services. Using JagrukSeva you can find various government schemes, check your eligibility, and apply for the schemes.
How JagrukSeva will help common citizens?
JagrukSeva will reduce the time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes.The easy user interface of JagrukSeva helps common people a lot in discovering and applying for schemes.
Can I apply for the schemes through JagrukSeva?
For now, the platform shall direct you to the application page of the scheme of your choice.It is envisaged that in the upcoming phases, JagrukSeva shall have the feature to apply for schemes / services from within the platform/ app.
